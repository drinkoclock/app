Drink O'Clock

Casino Royale

Sour Glass

Ingredients

2 cl
Vodka
2 cl
Creme de Banane
Grenadine
Orange juice

Instructions

  1. In a shaker half-filled with ice cubes, combine all of the ingredients.
  2. Put in mouth.
  3. Repeat as deemed necessary.
